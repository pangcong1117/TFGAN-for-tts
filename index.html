A Unified Metric-GAN Based Speech Enhancement Framework For TTS

Cong Pang

Abstract

Modern speech enhancement (SE) networks typically implement noise suppression through time-frequency masking, latent representation masking, or discriminative signal prediction. In this work, we propose a unified metric-GAN based speech enhancement framework for speech synthesis. We conducted experiments on the WenetSpeech Dataset, and the results demonstrate that our proposed method outperforms other state-of-the-art approaches in terms of objective metrics while causing less degradation to the speech.

1.WenetSpeech Dataset (TV series clips)

10000+ hours multi-domain transcribed Mandarin Speech Corpus collected from YouTube and Podcast. Optical character recognition (OCR) and automatic speech recognition (ASR) techniques are adopted to label each YouTube and Podcast recording, respectively. To improve the quality of the corpus, we use a novel end-to-end label error detection method to further validate and filter the data.
